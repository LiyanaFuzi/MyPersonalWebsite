MY EXPERIENCE ON REGISTER IN UiTM KEDAH

The view behind my college

This is my first time ever to stay far from my family and it feel sad to leave my beloved family to further my study at UiTM. My feelings were mixed while on the way to the UiTM campus on the day of registration. I am taking Diploma in Information Management (IM110) in UiTM Kedah. First of all I did not know what this course is about, but, after doing some research, I understand that this course are about management in Information Agency. They also include some subject that mandatory to take for 3 semester which is Integrated Language Skills (ELC) and Fundamentals of Islam (CTU).

As soon as we arrive at the college to register my room and give the documents that already provided that need to be brought along. While I was queuing, I saw my schoolmates that also apply to UiTM Kedah. After a long waited, I arrive at the registration table to give the documents needed and get my room key.
